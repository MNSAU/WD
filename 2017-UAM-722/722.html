Sandwich

A sandwich is a food typically consisting of vegetables, sliced cheese or meat, placed on or between slices of bread, or more generally any dish wherein bread serves as a container or wrapper for another food type. I always eat a sandwich because I like a sandwich. It is the best food in the world. Easy to make. Not Time Consuming.

Ingredients:

  • Sliced sandwich bread
  • Favorite sandwich sliced meat
  • Favorite cheese
  • Lettuce
  • Tomatoes
  • Pickles
  • Knives (1 for slicing, and 1 for spreading condiments)
  • Plate
  • Paper towels

Steps:

  1. Rinse all vegetables with clean water and dry them with a paper towel.
  2. Take out two slices of your favorite bread.
  3. You can mix some of the condiments together to try a variety of different flavors.
  4. Make sure to spread the meat in a way will go all the way to the edge of the bread.
  5. Make sure add enough cheese to cover the entire sandwich.
  6. Place the second slice of bread with the condiment covered side down on top of your toppings.
  7. If desired, slice sandwich into a shape that will make it easier to handle
